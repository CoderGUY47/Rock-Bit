Agentic AI Project Requirements

Build a complete, production-ready Full Stack Agentic AI Application using modern web technologies and Large Language Models (LLMs). The project must demonstrate your understanding of full-stack development, AI integration, agent workflows, prompt engineering, secure API design, database management, authentication, authorization, and professional UI/UX.



The objective is to build a polished, real-world AI application that combines excellent engineering practices with practical AI capabilities.



Note: Build a completely new project. Do not reuse, clone, or extend any of your previous projects. Choose a unique project idea that is clearly different in context and functionality from your earlier work or assignments.

2. Global UI & Design Rules
Use a maximum of 3 primary colors (+ optional neutral color).
Maintain consistent layout, spacing, and alignment throughout the project.
All cards and components must have the same size, border radius, and visual style.
Fully responsive for mobile, tablet, and desktop.
No placeholder or dummy content allowed.

Footer
